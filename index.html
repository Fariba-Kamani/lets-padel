Let's Padel!

Be a part of our growing community for padel lovers in northern Stockholm.

Find other padel players with Let's Padel!

Are you in the mood for playing some padel but you can't motivate your family or friends to join you and play?

Here at Let's Padel whether you want to play casually or you want to play matches to improve your game, we can help you find other padel players to connect and play padel with.

All you need to do is to fill in a form to tell us about your prefered date, time, place and skill level and wait. We will let you know as soon as your match is full and ready for you to play.

Benefits of playing padel

  • Helps to stay fit & healthy
  • Reduces stress
  • Enhances cognitive skills
  • Improves coordination, mobility & reflexes
  • Provides a platform to meet new people and make new friends
